Chips's Recipe

There's not much better than a side of golden-brown crispy chips.
We guarantee you'll love this method for making easy homemade fries.
In less than one hour and with only four household ingredients, you'll get
restuarant-quality chips or fries.

Incredient

  • Potatoes
  • Oil
  • Salt
  • Marinate sauce

How to make

  1. Slice potatoes into thick stick
  2. Soak the Potatoes
  3. Fry the Potatoes- twice
  4. Season the Fries
