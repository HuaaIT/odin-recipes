Fired Chicken's Recipe

Korean fried chicken is known for it's ultra crispiness that last for an extended
amount of time longer than regular fried chicken. And that is something we all love
But besides being super crisp, Korean fried chicken is known to be paired with
some or a the most addictive sauces! Soy garlic sauce and sweet and spicy gochujang
sauce being the most popular.

Incredient

  • Chicken wings
  • Water
  • Salt and sugar
  • Onion and garlic powder
  • Baking Powder
  • Gochugaru
  • Corn starch
  • Ginger powder
  • Flour

How to make

  1. Marinate wings
  2. Make the wet batter
  3. Prepare fry station
  4. Batter the wings
  5. First fry
  6. Second fry
  7. Combine soy sauce, corn syrup, sugar, galic and gochugaru
